    General rules

    • Prefix S2D01_ = your presets always stay at the top of the lists.

    • Same nomenclature in SD3 and Logic → zero confusion.

    • Folders divided by function (Drums, FX Bus, FX Texture, FX Vox, Master).

    • Update them as we add new "recipes".

    -> So you have a real nice ecosystem**: SD3 and Logic speak the same language, and you recall any chain/kit in 2 clicks.
