    Massive Attack Kit

    1. Kick (fundamental)

    • Basic: EZX2_HipHop → choose a dark, round, no-click kick.

    • Layer: DreamPop kick → adds soft body.

    • Processing in Logic:

      • EQ: boost at 60 Hz (+3 dB), cut at 250 Hz (-2/3 dB).

      • Slow comp (attack 30 ms, release 100 ms).

      • If you want electronic beat → add a sub-kick from the EZX_Electronic, filtered LPF 100 Hz.

    2. Snare

    • Base: DreamPop snare → velvety, dark.

    • Layer: dry clap/snare from the EZX2_HipHop → only on the highs (HPF 800 Hz).

    • Optional: a rimshot from EZX2_Reggae for "dub" color.

    • Processing in Logic:

      • EQ: low cut 120 Hz, boost 200 Hz (+2 dB), presence +3 dB at 2.5-3 kHz.

      • Light saturation (Overdrive Drive 2 dB).

      • Room: sends snare into the Room of HansaRooms → loud compression (trip-hop style pump).

    3. Hi-Hat & Cymbals

    • Get HH from DreamPop or HipHop → turn down volume, high cut 8-9 kHz.

    • Overheads → almost nothing.

    • Processing: short ping-pong delay (1/16), low feedback.

    4. Room & Ambience

    • Use HansaRooms → one of the large rooms.

    • Raise the Room more than the norm (it becomes part of the sound).

    • Compressor FET 1176, ratio 8:1, fast attack, medium release → pump.

    • High Cut 6 kHz to keep it dark.

    5. Extra percussions

    • Add from EZX Percussion (dark shaker, filtered drum).

    • Treat them with slow tremolo or tape delay → hypnotic groove.

    6. Bus Drums Processing

    • Glue Comp (VCA, ratio 2:1, attack 30 ms, release 100 ms, GR 2-3 dB).

    • Parallel Punch (see your Saturn/1176 chain).

    • Send to your Reverbs Ambient and Textures Shimmer buses to put them in the space.

    Final Character

    • Deep, dark kick with electronic sub underneath.

    • Velvety snare + dry clap → hypnotic groove.

    • Hi-hat filtered, almost hidden.

    • Compressed room that "breathes."

    • Minimal percussion pulsing.

      -> Result = Massive Attack style without buying more libraries.
