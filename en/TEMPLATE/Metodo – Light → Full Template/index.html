    Method: switch from Template Light → Full

    1. Start from the Light project.

    • You have SD3 Multi-Out + Bus Drums + Master.

    • No FX bus loaded.

    2. Recall your saved Channel Strip Settings.

    1. Create new Aux → Bus 20, 21, 22, etc. (as many as needed).

    2. On each one load your preset:

      • S2D01_Reverbs_Ambient.

      • S2D01_Textures_Shimmer

      • S2D01_Textures_ExtraDark

      • S2D01_Vox_TripHopAmbient

    3. Add sends on the Drums bus

    • Open the channel strip of the Bus Drums.

    • Activate the sends to the new Aux (Reverbs, Textures, Vox).

    • Set initial levels low (-20 dB) and adjust by ear.

    4. Master unchanged

    • The Master Out already remains with your setting (Ambient or Punchy).

    • No need to redo anything.

    Result

    • In 3 minutes turn the Light Template into Full Template.

    • You didn't have to re-route SD3 or move tracks.

    • It's a modular system: -> if you just need a reverb, you just load that.
      -> if you want the whole architecture, you load all the buses.
