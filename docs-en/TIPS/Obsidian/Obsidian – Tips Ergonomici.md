# Obsidian - Ergonomic Tips

## 1. Quick navigation.
- **Important note pins**: open the note (e.g. `00_INDEX`) and click the thumbtack in the upper right ‚Üí it always stays fixed.
- **Sidebar ON/OFF**: open/close by clicking icons, or you can assign hotkeys in `Settings ‚Üí Hotkeys`:
  - Toggle left sidebar = shortcut of your choice
  - Toggle right sidebar = shortcut of your choice

## 2. Minimal formatting in Markdown.
- `# Title` ‚Üí large title
- `## Subtitle` ‚Üí medium title
- `- List` ‚Üí bulleted list
- `[NameNote](NameNote.md)` ‚Üí link to other note

*(Nothing else is really needed!)*

## 3. Section covers.
For each important folder (e.g., Harmonial, Superior Drummer 3) create a **note-cover page** with the same name.

## 5. Starred Notes
- You can mark your most used notes as "Starred" (you need the **Starred** core plugin).
- So you always keep DRUMS, MASTER, FX, etc. at hand.
